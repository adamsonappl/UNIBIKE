ABOUT US

An innovative and eco-friendly bike-sharing solution at Caraga State University. Our mission is to provide a convenient, sustainable, and inclusive mode of transportation for students and faculty, aligning with global efforts to promote environmental sustainability and well-being.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being – By promoting cycling as a healthy and accessible mode of transportation, we encourage physical activity, which contributes to better overall health and well-being.
  • SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities – Our system reduces carbon emissions and traffic congestion, supporting the creation of more sustainable and resilient urban spaces.
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production – By optimizing the use of shared resources, we minimize waste and promote a circular economy.
  • SDG 13: Climate Action – Our bike-sharing program reduces reliance on fossil-fueled transportation, helping to mitigate climate change by lowering the campus’s carbon footprint.
